Role handoff model
  • HR Recruiter/Company Admin creates requisition
  • Hiring Manager approves and publishes
  • HR Recruiter screens and schedules interviews
  • Interviewer submits scorecard and finalizes
  • HR Recruiter selects/rejects and sends offer
  • Hiring Manager confirms final hire after acceptance

Platform Features

Everything needed to run modern recruitment operations with transparency, speed, and quality.

Requisition and Approval Flow

Draft jobs, submit for manager approval, publish, and close with tracked ownership.

Application Stage Pipeline

Track stages from applied to screening, interview, selected/rejected, offer, and hired.

Interview Assignment and Scheduling

Assign interviewer, schedule interviews, and track upcoming/completed interview states.

Structured Scorecards

Offer and Hire Workflow

Role-Based Experience

Each user role gets focused capabilities without unnecessary complexity.

Platform Admin

  • Create and manage companies
  • Provision company admins
  • Monitor global audit logs
  • Review contact submissions
  • Govern access and lifecycle

Company Admin

  • Create HR, Manager, Interviewer users
  • Manage company jobs and offers
  • Track applications by job
  • Review company-scoped activity logs

HR Recruiter

  • Create drafts and submit jobs
  • Screen applications and candidates
  • Assign/schedule interviewers
  • Send offers and update decisions

Hiring Manager

  • Approve/reject job requests
  • Publish and close approved jobs
  • Review accepted offers
  • Make final hired/rejected decision

Interviewer

  • View assigned interviews
  • Submit detailed scorecards
  • Finalize score and mark completion
  • Send score outcome to HR

Candidate

  • Create candidate account
  • Browse jobs
  • Apply for jobs
  • Track live application status
  • Manage profile and resume
  • Accept or decline offers
